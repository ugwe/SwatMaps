Welcome to SwatMaps!

This is a student-built WebApp to help you navigate Swarthmore's campus. You can view a live map of the campus or search for a specific location on our campus map.

SwatMaps is a live-updated service. We encourage users to give us suggestions about features and content that they would like to see implemented. Users can send us suggestions and compliments using our input page!

SwatMaps was created as a final project for CPSC 071.1 in Fall of 2023. This project was made by Mehtap Yercel, Tina Chen, Michael Ugwe, and Rain White in consultation with our Professor Michael Wehar.

Our floorplans were drawn using references provided by a representative via Swarthmore College's Capital Planning and Project Management Department of Sustainable Facilities Operations and Capital Planning.

Libraries and plugins we used: Leaflet, Leaflet Awesome Markers, Leaflet Locate, Leaflet Side Bar.

Our GitHub is available here. We hope you enjoy this service! If you have any feedback, questions, comments, or concerns about SwatMaps, we encourage you to keep them to yourself :)
